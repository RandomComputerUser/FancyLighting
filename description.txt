Fancy Lighting improves the game's lighting and graphics!

Features:
- Smooth lighting
- Ambient occlusion
- Accurate shadows
- Improved sky rendering
- Simulated normal maps
- HDR rendering (tone mapped to SDR)
- Bloom and depth of field
- Camera mode compatibility

For the best performance, try lowering the in-game resolution to either 1920x1080 or 1920x1200 depending on the aspect ratio of your display.

IMPORTANT: If you enter a world with this mod disabled and then later enable this mod, there may be graphical glitches. It is recommended to have this mod enabled before entering any worlds after launching tModLoader.
